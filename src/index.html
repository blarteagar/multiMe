Timeline of Mandela's Life

1918 Born into the Thembu royal family in Mvezo, Union of South Africa.

1943 Joined the African National Congress (ANC) party

1944 Co-founded ANC Youth League

1962 Sentenced to life imprisonment for conspiring to overthrow the state following the Rivonia Trial

1990 President F. W. de Klerk released Nelson Mandela

1990 Mandela and de Klerk led efforts to negotiate an end to apartheid

1993 Nelson Mandela and F.W. De Clerk awarded 1993 Nobel Peace Prize for momentous contribution to peaceful elimination of apartheid in South Africa.

1994 Multiracial general election in which Mandela led the ANC to victory and became president. Served as President of South Africa from 1994 to 1999

2013 Death
